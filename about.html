🍕 Barbeque Pizza 🍕

This popular pizza place has many tasty kinds of pizza, from classic cheese to special flavors. There are two locations: one in Jericho and Ramallah. They use fresh ingredients and also have salads and appetizers. The staff is friendly, and the place is cozy, making it great for families and friends. You can also get delivery or takeout to enjoy their pizza at home. With great service and a big menu, it's a top choice for pizza lovers in the area.
